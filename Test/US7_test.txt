Modify Listings

-User removes a listing from their account

Test Case:

-The user must be logged into their account.

-The user should have at least one listing associated with their account.
    -User logs into their account.
    -User navigates to the "My Listings" or "Manage Listings" section of their account.
    -User locates the listing they want to remove.
    -User clicks on the "Remove" button for that listing.
        -Listing is removed
    -User clicks on the "Update" button for that listing.
        -Modal prompts user for new data
        -Listing is modified

-Verify persistence of data:
    -Log out of the user's account.
    -Log back in
